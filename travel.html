Transportation

By Car

Driving is the recommended method for reaching Hawick, which is easily accessible via the A7 or A68. The venue is located west of Hawick, with the turn-in on the right when coming from the town.

Please note: Parking at the venue is very limited and must be arranged with us in advance. We recommend taking taxis between Hawick and the venue.

By Public Transport

Carlisle Station
Train connections from England. X95 bus service to Hawick available.

Galashiels Station
The closest station to Hawick. Regular services from Edinburgh. X95 bus service to Hawick available.

Accommodation

The venue has three nearby apartments available to rent on a first-come, first-served basis.


Townhouse Apartment

Sleeps: up to 10
Bedrooms: 2 double rooms, 2 family rooms (1 double and 2 single beds), and 1 twin room

Rates: £400 (1st night), £350 (2nd night), £300 (3rd night)

*RESERVED*


Teviot Apartment

Sleeps: up to 6
Bedrooms: 2 double rooms and a sofa bed in living room

Rates: £200 (1st night), £175 (2nd night), £100 (3rd night)


Tweed Apartment

Sleeps: up to 4
Bedrooms: 1 double room and a sofa bed in living room

Rates: £130 (1st night), £120 (2nd night), £75 (3rd night)

For further information, visit the Branxholm Park website or contact directly at info@branxholmpark.co.uk for availability and bookings.

Hawick also offers a range of hotels and guest houses within easy reach of the venue.
