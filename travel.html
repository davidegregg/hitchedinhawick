Getting There

By Air

Edinburgh Airport (EDI) - 1 hour drive
The closest major international airport with excellent connections from worldwide destinations.

Newcastle Airport (NCL) - 1.5 hours drive
Alternative option with good UK and European connections.

By Train

Carlisle Station - 45 minutes drive
Regular trains from London, Edinburgh, and Glasgow connect to Carlisle.

Edinburgh Waverley - 1.5 hours drive
Scotland's main railway hub with connections across the UK.

By Car

From Edinburgh: A7 south via Galashiels (1 hour)
From Newcastle: A68 north through Jedburgh (1.5 hours)
From Glasgow: A702 south via Biggar (1.5 hours)

Parking available on-site at Branxholm Park House.
