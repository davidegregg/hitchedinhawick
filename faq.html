Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive by 13:00 for the drinks reception. The ceremony begins at 14:00 sharp, so we recommend arriving with a few minutes to spare to find your seat and settle in.

When is the celebration expected to finish?

Music and entertainment will wind down at midnight, with taxis arranged to arrive at 12:30am to ensure all non-residents depart by 1am (as per venue requirements). If you're staying locally, we recommend arranging transport or accommodation in advance!

What should I wear?

We'd love you to dress to impress! Please refer to the attire guidance and color palette included with your invitation. Given it's October in Scotland, we recommend bringing a jacket or wrap for cooler moments.

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

Whilst the ceremony will be held indoors, the venue does feature a variety of outdoor social spaces that we plan to enjoy throughout the day (weather permitting). Not all of these are covered, so we recommend bringing an umbrella just in case!

Can I take photographs during the ceremony?

We'd prefer an unplugged ceremony so everyone can be fully present for our vows. Our photographer will capture all the special moments to share with you later. Feel free to take photos during the reception and party!

Can I share photos to social media?

We'd love for you to share your photos from our special day! However, we kindly ask that you wait until we've had the chance to share our own photos first. We'll let everyone know when we're happy for social media sharing to begin.

Is smoking or vaping permitted?

In line with venue policy, smoking and vaping are only permitted in designated outdoor areas. Please ask a member of staff if you need directions to these areas.

Where exactly is Branxholm Park House?

Branxholm Park House
Hawick TD9 0JT, Scotland
The venue is located about 3 miles south of Hawick town center on the A7. Look for the grand stone gates and tree-lined driveway.

Is parking available at the venue?

Parking at the venue is very limited and must be arranged with us in advance. We recommend taking taxis between Hawick and the venue.

Will I need to travel between different venues during the day?

All events take place at Branxholm Park House, so no transport between venues is needed! Just a pleasant stroll around the beautiful grounds between different areas of the estate.

Are all areas wheelchair accessible?

The main areas we'll be using are wheelchair accessible, including the chapel, orangery, and main house. If you have specific accessibility needs, please let us know when you RSVP so we can ensure everything is arranged for you.

Do you have any dietary requirements or food allergies?

Please let us know about any dietary requirements, food allergies, or intolerances when you RSVP. We want to ensure everyone can enjoy the meal safely and comfortably. Our caterers can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specific dietary needs.

Will there be an open bar, or can I bring my own drinks?

We'll be stocking and providing a full open bar for the day, so there's no need to bring any drinks along. As per venue policy, outside alcohol isn't permitted on the grounds.

That said, since we're putting the bar together ourselves, we'd love to hear if you have a favourite tipple — if there's something you particularly enjoy, let us know in advance and we'll do our best to make sure there's a bottle behind the bar for you!

When do I need to RSVP by?

Please respond by February 1st, 2026. This gives us time to finalize catering numbers and seating arrangements. We kindly ask that each guest completes their own individual RSVP — this helps us keep track of everyone's preferences and requirements accurately.

Can I bring a plus-one?

We're keeping our celebration intimate with close friends and family. Plus-ones have been specified on individual invitations. If you're unsure whether your invitation includes a guest, please feel free to reach out to us.

Are children invited?

We've decided to make our wedding an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives all our guests the opportunity to relax and enjoy the day. We appreciate your understanding!

How do I make changes after I RSVP?

Life happens! If your plans change after you've already responded, just send us an email as soon as possible and we'll update our records.

Do you have a wedding registry?

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift of all! If you'd like to contribute toward our future together, you can find details on our RSVP page. Cash gifts are also warmly appreciated and can be brought on the day.

Can I bring a gift to the wedding?

While not necessary, if you'd like to bring a card or gift to the wedding, we'll have a designated table set up during the reception where you can leave them safely.

How should I address the married couple after the wedding?

We've chosen not to follow the usual tradition of the bride taking the groom's last name. Instead, we're both holding onto our own surnames as we start this new chapter together. Please feel free to refer to us however feels natural — David Gregg and Emma-jo Sinclair, or simply David & Emma-jo!
